Ingredients:
 - 1 tsp sugar
 - 4 cups bread flour
 - 1 and 1/2 tbsp olive oil
 - 1 and 3/8 cups water
 - 1 and 1/2 tsp salt
 - 2 tsp active dry yeast

Directions:
 1. Measure and add liquid ingredients to the bread pan.

 2. Measure and add dry ingredients to the bread pan, except the yeast.
 
 3. Add the yeast on the top of the dry ingredients.
 
 4. Start the bread maker.
